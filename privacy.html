1. Global Data Practices (Architecture & Scope)

Vectixore LTD operates a diverse portfolio of applications ranging from general utility tools to high-security forensic solutions. Our approach to data privacy is adaptive to the specific purpose of each application:

  • General Utility Products: For our standard applications, we strictly adhere to Data Minimization principles, processing only the absolute minimum data required for functionality.
  • High-Assurance & Forensic Products: For applications requiring legal validity, identity verification, or cryptographic sealing (such as PocketNotary), we process sensitive and biometric data under strict forensic protocols.

By using our Services, you acknowledge that the following data practices apply where relevant to the specific functionality of the application being used.

1.1. Handwritten Signature Data

Where applicable, we collect the visual representation (image) and vector paths (stroke data) of the signature you draw on the screen.

  • Purpose: This data is processed strictly as "Document Content" to be embedded into the PDF.
  • Disclaimer: Vectixore LTD does NOT perform graphological analysis or dynamic biometric verification (pressure/speed analysis) on this signature. We only cryptographically seal the image provided.

1.2. Identity Document & NFC Data (High-Assurance)

To achieve "Substantial Assurance" (NIST IAL2 / eIDAS High) levels of identity verification in our secure applications, the App accesses the NFC controller of your device to communicate with the RFID chip embedded in your government-issued Identity Document.

A. Data Extracted via OCR:

We scan the "Machine Readable Zone" to extract Document Number, Expiry Date, Date of Birth, and Issuing State. Purpose: To unlock the encrypted NFC chip.

B. Data Extracted via NFC (Chip Read):
  • DG1: High-fidelity text data (Full Name, Document Type).
  • DG2: The high-resolution, digitally signed biometric facial image.
  • SOD: The digital signature of the issuing government (CSCA).

Purpose: Anti-Forgery validation against ICAO PKD and Face Matching against your Live Selfie.

1.3. Biometric Data (Liveness & Matching)

  • Forensic Face Photo: We capture a visual photograph of your face at the moment of signing/verification. This is compared (1:1) against the trusted NFC Chip image.
  • Liveness Detection: We analyze the video stream for micro-movements and depth (Presentation Attack Detection).
  • Local Auth: We use device sensors (Fingerprint/FaceID) as a secondary layer. We DO NOT receive raw templates from the OS.

1.4. Permanent Audit Trail (Hash Registry)

For services involving document integrity, we retain the SHA-256 Hash (Digital Fingerprint) and Server Timestamp indefinitely. This validates authenticity without storing the file itself.

1.5. Virtual Goods Data (Stamina)

We process your "Stamina" balance and transaction history. Stamina is a virtual license with no real-world monetary value and cannot be exchanged for cash.

1.6. Phone Number & Identity Binding

Collected strictly for SMS-based verification (OTP) and 2FA. We cryptographically link the verified Phone Number to the Verified ID Document to create a persistent "Mobile Identity."

1.7. Network & Device Data

  • IP Address: For security logs and fraud prevention.
  • Device Integrity: Model, Hardware IDs, and Root/Jailbreak status to verify the signing environment.
  • Advertising ID: Solely for analytics and fraud prevention.

1.8. Location & Motion Data

  • GPS: Processed locally to generate a static map snapshot for the forensic seal.
  • Motion Sensors: Gyroscope/Accelerometer data processed locally for "Proof of Human" verification.

1.9. Referral & Interaction Data

Processed strictly to distribute "Stamina" rewards in our "Invite a Friend" program. We do not access contacts without explicit permission.

1.10. Customer Support & Diagnostics

We collect email, device info, crash logs, and thermal state via Firebase Crashlytics to resolve issues and improve stability.

1.11. Cloud Relay (Transient Storage)

When using "Cloud Relay," files are stored as AES-256 Encrypted Binary Blobs on Google Cloud/Firebase. Vectixore LTD does not possess decryption keys. Files are automatically deleted 7 days after upload.

1.12. Inactive Account Policy

We reserve the right to delete accounts inactive for 24 months. The Hash Registry is exempt to ensure legal validity.

1.13. Children's Privacy

Our Services are not intended for anyone under 13 (or 16 in certain jurisdictions). We do not knowingly collect data from children.

1.14. Roles & Responsibilities

  • Vectixore LTD: Data Controller for Account Data (Phone, IP, Logs, Stamina).
  • You (The User): Data Controller for the Content of your documents. We act strictly as a Data Processor.

1.15. Do Not Track (DNT)

Our systems do not currently respond to DNT signals as no uniform standard exists.
