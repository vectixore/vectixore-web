Privacy Policy & Data Governance Framework

01. Introduction

Vectixore LTD ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or use our services, in strict compliance with the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR) and the Data Protection Act 2018.

02. Data We Collect

We may collect and process the following categories of personal data required for our business operations:

  • Identity & Contact Data: Name, email address, and professional details provided via inquiries.
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, operating system, and device information collected automatically.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with our website and digital services.

03. Legal Basis for Processing

Under UK GDPR Article 6, we rely on the following lawful bases:

  • Contractual Necessity: To fulfill our service obligations to you.
  • Legitimate Interests: For network security, product development, and business operations.
  • Legal Obligation: To comply with UK corporate and tax laws.

04. Data Storage & International Transfers

Vectixore LTD is incorporated in England & Wales. Your data is stored on secure servers. We do not transfer data outside the UK unless adequate protective measures (such as Standard Contractual Clauses or UK Adequacy Regulations) are in place.

05. Your Legal Rights

You retain full control over your data. Under UK GDPR, you have the right to access, correct, erase ("right to be forgotten"), restrict processing, and object to the processing of your personal data.
