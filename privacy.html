1. GLOBAL DATA PRACTICES (ARCHITECTURE)

Vectixore LTD operates a diverse portfolio of applications ranging from general utility tools to high-security forensic solutions. Our approach to data privacy is adaptive to the specific purpose of each application:

  • General Utility Products: For our standard applications, we strictly adhere to Data Minimization principles, processing only the absolute minimum data required for functionality.
  • High-Assurance & Forensic Products: For applications requiring legal validity, identity verification, or cryptographic sealing (such as DoubtProof‚Ñ¢), we process sensitive and biometric data under strict forensic protocols.

By using our Services, you acknowledge that the following data practices apply where relevant to the specific functionality of the application being used.

1.1. Handwritten Signature Data

Where applicable, we collect the visual representation (image) and vector paths (stroke data) of the signature you draw on the screen.

  • Purpose: This data is processed strictly as "Document Content" to be embedded into the PDF.
  • Disclaimer: Vectixore LTD does NOT perform graphological analysis or dynamic biometric verification (pressure/speed analysis) on this signature. We only cryptographically seal the image provided.

1.2. Identity Document & NFC Data (High-Assurance Verification)

To achieve "Substantial Assurance" (NIST IAL2 / eIDAS High) levels of identity verification in our secure applications, the App accesses the NFC controller of your device to communicate with the RFID chip embedded in your government-issued Identity Document.

A. Data Extracted via OCR (Optical Character Recognition):

  • MRZ Data: We scan the "Machine Readable Zone" to extract Document Number, Expiry Date, Date of Birth, and Issuing State.
  • Purpose: To unlock the encrypted NFC chip (PACE/BAC protocols).

B. Data Extracted via NFC (Chip Read):

  • DG1 & DG2: Text data and high-resolution biometric facial images stored by the issuing government.
  • Purpose: Anti-Forgery validation and ownership verification via 1:1 Face Matching.

1.3. Biometric Data (Liveness & Matching)

We process biometric signals strictly for identity verification. We do NOT receive raw biometric templates (Fingerprint/FaceID) from the device OS; we only receive an authentication signal.

1.4. Permanent Audit Trail (Hash Registry)

We retain the SHA-256 Hash and the Server Timestamp of processed documents indefinitely to ensure legal validity and proof of existence.

1.5. Virtual Goods Data (Stamina)

We process your "Stamina" balance. This is a virtual license with no real-world monetary value and cannot be refunded or exchanged for cash.

1.6. Phone Number & Identity Binding

Phone numbers are collected for SMS-based verification (OTP) and cryptographically linked to Verified IDs for persistent security.

1.7. Network & Device Data

We collect IP addresses and hardware identifiers strictly for security logs, fraud prevention (detecting Stamina abuse), and ensuring device integrity (Root/Jailbreak detection).

1.8. Location & Motion Data

GPS coordinates are processed locally for forensic seals. Motion sensors (Gyro/Accel) are used locally for "Proof of Human" verification.

1.9. Diagnostics & Cloud Relay

Crash logs are collected via Firebase Crashlytics. Encrypted files in "Cloud Relay" are stored as AES-256 blobs and automatically deleted after 7 days.
